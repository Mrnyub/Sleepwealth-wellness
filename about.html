Our Heritage

A Sanctuary for the Restless.

Founded on the principle that rest is a necessity, not a luxury, Sleepwealth was born out of the modern epidemic of burnout.

We believe in providing a haven where professionals, students, and travelers can disconnect from the noise and reconnect with their inner calm.

Our Mission

To provide a relaxing and comfortable place for students who have vacant time, workers, and working students who need rest and peace of mind.

Our Vision

To become a well-known wellness lounge that helps workers and working students relax, reduce stress, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
