Our Origins

The Art of Doing Nothing.

In a hyper-connected world that glorifies the hustle, true rest has become the ultimate luxury. Sleepwealth was founded as an antidote to modern burnout.

Our Mission

To provide a relaxing and comfortable place for students who have vacant time, workers, and working students who need rest and peace of mind.

Our Vision

To become a well-known wellness lounge that helps workers and working students relax, reduce stress, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
